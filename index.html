Bio.  I am a PhD student from the Smart Microgrids Team at the University of Liège, supported by the Belgian National Fund for Scientific Research (FNRS). My PhD thesis, under the supervision of Prof. Bertrand Cornélusse, focuses on examining the impact of the energy transition on electricity distribution networks. Specifically, I am developing a co-optimization framework for planning low-voltage distribution networks. My research interests include mathematical optimization, game theory, distribution network planning, and power system applications and modeling.
